About ME

Background

I've always been curious about having a more technical career and here I am! For starters, I have my BS in Marketing from Umass Dartmouth and a certificate in Web Design/Development from Bristol Community College. Currently I am working on my MS in CIS from Boston University.

I spent two years as a marketing coordinator and discovered the world of programming. Then I just had to dive in! Overall being creative, growth focused, a problem solver, and innovative is what really drives me as a marketer and/or developer. Currently looking to hone my skills as a developer; Both front-end and eventually back-end, using my marketing, design, and analytical skills as compliments.

My interest is in learning what makes great products and being part of a productive team. When I am not coding or reading up on marketing/tech news, I enjoy spending time with family and friends, playing some video games, and following/playing sports. Upon completion of my masters or depending on my availability, I am always interested in freelance marketing or development opportunities.
